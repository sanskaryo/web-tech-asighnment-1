Welcome to Mathura!

Mathura is a holy city in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is the birthplace of Lord Krishna, one of the most important deities in Hinduism. The city is home to many temples and shrines dedicated to Lord Krishna, as well as other Hindu deities. Mathura is also a popular pilgrimage destination for Hindus from all over the world.

Here are some of the reasons why you should visit Mathura:

  • To experience the rich Hindu culture and heritage of the city.
  • To visit the many temples and shrines dedicated to Krishna.
  • To take a walk along the Yamuna River, which flows through the city.
  • To enjoy the delicious food that Mathura is known for.
